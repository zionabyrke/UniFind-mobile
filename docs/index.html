About the Project

What is UniFind?

UniFind was built to make student-to-student transactions personalized, faster, more organized, and safer within the BU campus, while promoting affordability and sustainability through item reuse. Only verified BU students with an active @bicol-u.edu.ph Google account can access the app.

Mission

To provide students with a trusted digital marketplace that makes buying and selling easier, safer, and more accessible.

Vision

To become the go-to campus marketplace where every BU student can connect, trade, and support one another within the university community.

How It Works

From sign-in to transaction

Checkout does not finalize a transaction, it creates an order request. The seller accepts or rejects it, then buyer and seller coordinate payment and meetup through the in-app chat.

01

Sign in with your BU Google account

Only @bicol-u.edu.ph emails are accepted. Non-BU accounts are blocked at the server level before account creation.

02

Browse or post listings

Scroll on the Home feed or search by category/keyword. Add items to your cart or post your own with photos, a description, and a price.

03

Send an order request

Checkout submits an order to the seller. The seller reviews it and accepts or rejects. No payment happens inside the app.

04

Coordinate and meet on campus

Use the built-in chat to arrange payment method and meetup location. After completion, both parties can leave a review.

Download

Installing UniFind

UniFind is not yet on the Google Play Store. Install directly using the APK file.

1
Download the latest APK from GitHub Releases.
2
On your device, go to Settings -> Install unknown apps and allow your browser or file manager to install APKs.
3
Open the downloaded file and tap Install.
4
Sign in with your @bicol-u.edu.ph Google account.
⚠️ Requires Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher. iOS is not supported.
